About Us

JKD NEXUS LIMITED is a professional trade and exchange company founded in 2025 by Abdulrahman Shehu Jakada. We are committed to simplifying cross-border business transactions and creating reliable connections between buyers and suppliers. The company is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Nigeria, under Registration Number: RC 8352657.

Our Services

(1) Goods Sourcing

We source all kinds of goods directly from factories, at high quality and affordable prices. This includes trucks, excavators, rollers, crushers and other construction machineries; used and brand-new automobiles; electric vehicles (EVs); tractors, harvesters and other agricultural equipment; building materials; electronics equipment and accessories; furniture; consumer goods; and any other type of goods you may need.

(2) Foreign Exchange Services

We provide instant and reliable RMB payment services, available 24/7, to support smooth and secure business transactions. Payments can be made via WeChat, Alipay, cash or bank transfer.
